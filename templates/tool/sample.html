Riverside Medical Center

$42M Hospital Expansion Procore + Primavera P6
Bad Estimate

A hospital expansion project suffering from a fundamentally flawed original estimate. Unit costs were below market from day one, and multiple scope items were missing from the bid documents. The CPI has been poor since the start of construction, with the most significant overruns in structural and MEP trades.

Summit Tower Mixed-Use

$28M Commercial Building Sage 300 CRE + MS Project
Operations + Change Failure

A mixed-use commercial tower where the estimate was reasonable, but execution has gone sideways. Labor productivity is poor, overtime is running high, and change orders are accumulating without formal approval. Multiple trades report receiving verbal direction to proceed, but the paperwork never follows. The pending CO backlog keeps growing.

Greenfield Distribution Center

$65M Industrial Facility Viewpoint Vista + Primavera P6
Conflict of Agency

A large distribution center where the GC is self-performing a disproportionate share of the work -- particularly concrete, sitework, and earthwork. Self-performed trades are consistently running over budget while subcontracted work stays on track. Change orders cluster suspiciously below approval thresholds, and markups on self-performed changes are notably higher.
